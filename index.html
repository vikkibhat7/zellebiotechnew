About us

Zelle Biotechnology, with its Head Office in Mumbai, is ideally located for business all over India. Mumbai being the financial and industrial capital of India serves as the perfect location for Zelle.

Formed in October 2004, Zelle Biotechnology started as an entrepreneurial venture for products and services to the Life Science and BioPharma markets in India.

Direct experience in the Biotechnology industry in USA and India places Zelle Biotech in a position to understand the critical requirements of their clients. Concept selling and appropriate technical support make Zelle Biotech the perfect partner to provide niche products and services to the Biotechnology and Drug Discovery companies in India.

We combine our knowledge, skill and ability to contribute value to every interaction with our customers.
